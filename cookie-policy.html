Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Thread-Core uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our site. They help us understand how the site is used and improve functionality.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for site functionality.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand website traffic.

Managing Cookies

You can adjust your browser settings to refuse or delete cookies. However, this may affect site functionality.
